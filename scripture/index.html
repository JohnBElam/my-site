Decision Gospel

The Canon of the Church of Sequential Decision Systems

These texts were not planned. They emerged from the accumulated frustration of watching organizations spend millions solving the wrong problem with the right tools. Read them as doctrine, as satire, or as both. The distinction matters less than you think.
