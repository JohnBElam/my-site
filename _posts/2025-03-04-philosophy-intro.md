---

Fixed plans are brittle. The moment demand shifts, a truck breaks down, or the weather turns, the plan is wrong. Decision systems that ship embrace uncertainty by design: they use **policies** — rules that map state and new information to actions — not static sequences.

This is the core of a practitioner-first view: build systems that *respond* to what actually happens, not what we hoped would happen. More to come in this space.
