About Me

Applied decision scientist with a focus on building systems that ship — not dashboards that sit. Co-founder of Bit Bros LLC, where we publish handbooks on optimization and career development, and consult on decision intelligence.

Multi-faceted: author, practitioner, and advocate for a Decision Systems Philosophy that puts uncertainty and real-world outcomes at the center. Not interested in models that live in notebooks; only systems that run in production and drive decisions.

The world doesn't need another dashboard. It needs decision systems that embrace uncertainty, run in production, and make money while you sleep.

Bit Bros
